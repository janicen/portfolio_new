YSC Logo

logo

Type

Freelance project

Role

Graphic artist

Tools

Objective

Design a logo for the 2012 Young Star Challenge talent show.

Process

With a new talent show competition at the TD Vancouver Chinatown Festival particularly targeting younger competitors of age 5 to 12, a new and playful logo had to be designed. Hence, I offered to help in illustrating a playful logo to encourage kids to participate. I first produced various sketches and researched colour schemes, as well as shapes that signifies child-play. Next, the logo was drawn in Adobe Illustrator, followed by editing the mascot in Adobe Photoshop and that childish typography has been used in order to relate to their environment.

Result

Since this was a new competition at the festival, there was a large respond at the audition and that chairmans were pleased with the outcome and has invited me to create further illustrations for other events.
